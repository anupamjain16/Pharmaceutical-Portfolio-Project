About Us

Our Philosophy

The Name “Medicis” The word is an ode to the Renaissance leader Cosimo de’Medici. It is often used when describing something as complete, good to go, straight, golden and legit. True to the name "MEDICIS" stands for high quality medicine for better health care.

Our Mission

The mission of Medicis Healthcare is to improve the efficiency of healthcare systems worldwide by providing them high quality products and services and contributing substantially towards well-being and health of the society.

Striving for a happier and healthier tomorrow, we are determined to provide a total customer satisfaction and achieve leadership in chosen market, products and services across the globe, through excellence in high quality.

Our Vision

To alleviate human suffering with excellent high quality affordable medicines created with a spirit of innovation, entrepreneurship & sustainability to create a better tomorrow and to become India’s leading company which creates value for all stakeholders offering excellence in product quality standards, service and commitment.
